Privacy Policy

We at DP Design Pilot are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines what data we collect and how we use it.

What data we collect

  • Email address – to contact you regarding early access to our product;
  • Survey responses – to help us improve the product based on user needs;
  • Optional comments – to better understand your expectations.

How we use your data

All information is used internally for the following purposes:

  • Improving DP Design Pilot’s features and functionality;
  • Notifying you when the product is ready or updated;
  • Providing you access to trial or full versions if requested.

Sharing with third parties

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. All data is securely stored and only accessible to the DP Design Pilot team.

Your rights

You may contact us at any time at dpavlov.dpdesign@gmail.com to:

  • Request a copy of your stored data;
  • Request correction or deletion of your data;
  • Withdraw your participation in product testing.
